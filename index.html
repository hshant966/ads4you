faq

Straight Answers. No Sales Talk.

How much does a project cost?
+
Pricing starts at ₹2,500 for smaller automations and scales with complexity, integrations, and timeline. You receive a fixed-price proposal before any work begins — no surprise invoices, ever.
How long does a project take?
+
Simple bots and single workflows: 3–7 working days. Mid-complexity automation systems: 1–2 weeks. Full website builds: 2–3 weeks. Exact timelines confirmed in your proposal and tracked throughout.
What is the payment structure?
+
50% advance to begin work. 50% on final delivery — after you've reviewed and approved the completed project. No full upfront payments. No hidden fees.
Is there support after delivery?
+
Yes. Every project includes a defined post-delivery support window. Bugs discovered during this period are fixed at no charge. After the window, ongoing support is available as a separate, transparent engagement.
How many revisions are included?
+
Two rounds of revisions are included. Revisions mean adjustments within the agreed scope — not scope changes or full redesigns. Additional scope is quoted separately and agreed in writing before we proceed.
What if I'm not satisfied with the work?
+
If the delivered work doesn't match the agreed proposal, we fix it — no argument, no charge. If the issue is unresolvable and the failure is ours, we assess a partial or full refund of the advance. We don't hide behind fine print.
Do you work with businesses outside Pune?
+
Yes. All work is delivered remotely. We're based in Wakad, Pune but serve clients across Maharashtra and India via WhatsApp, email, and video call.
Do you offer white-label services?
+
Yes. Open to white-label arrangements for agencies offering automation services under their own brand. Contact us to discuss scope, pricing, and NDA terms.
